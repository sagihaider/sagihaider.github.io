The study focused on developing seven regression models to predict gas composition and gas yield, employing an Explainable AI (XAI) method for interpretability. The gradient boosting algorithm outperformed other regression-based models. The application of SHAP (Shapley additive explanations) values was utilized to elucidate the impact of input variables on the target. The results suggest that XAI, particularly when coupled with the gradient boosting algorithm, serves as a valuable tool for enhancing decision-making processes in fluidized bed gasifiers, providing transparency and interpretability to the predictive models.
