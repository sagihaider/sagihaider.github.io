excerpt: '[SC'15] In traditional supervised learning, a common assumption is a consistent probability distribution between training and testing phases. This paper addresses covariate shifts, common in systems like EEG-based BCIs. It introduces a covariate shift-detection and -adaptation method, using an EWMA model. Evaluation on BCI datasets shows significant accuracy improvement.'
