Delivered a Keynote speech at IEEE International Conference on Computing, Communication, and Intelligent Systems (Co-Sponsored by IEEE) organised by School of Engineering and Technology, Sharda University, Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India held on 19th-20th February 2021.

This talk was focused on Brain-Computer interfacing. Brain-computer interface (BCI) is a collaboration between a brain and a device that enables signals from the brain to direct some external activity, such as control of a cursor or a prosthetic limb. The interface enables a direct communications pathway between the brain and the object to be controlled. I presented  my research done during my PhD, Post-Doc, and Essex.
