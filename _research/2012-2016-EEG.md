From a Vision to Reality! Brain-Computer Interface based robotic therapy for hand rehabilitation after stroke tested at the Regency Hospital Kanpur on chronic stroke patients. A homegrown technology dedicated to the service of mankind by a group of Indian Researchers at the IIT Kanpur Robotics Lab and ISRC, Ulster University.

* Feature extraction and signal processing of EEG and EMG signals using band-pass and spatial filters. 

* A single-trial EEG classification method has been proposed for motor execution based on novel covariate shift-adaption. 

*  Experiments have been performed on healthy and stroke subjects and their results are their results were analysed.
